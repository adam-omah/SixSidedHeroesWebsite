Six Sided Heroes

Epic Dice RPG for Mobile.

Six Sided Heroes is a Andriod Mobile RPG game based around Dice Battles. Your Heroes face a multitude of different enemies in fast paced Dice battles.

Gather the greatest Heroes in the realm and push back the forces of evil! You can have a total of 5 Heroes on your team each with different abilities and skills to attack, defend and heal.

Upgrade Your Heroes

Gather Epic Items

Use the Item Shop to upgrade your heroes, buy from a selection of over 100 different Weapons and Armours!

Gain Gold

Earn gold from every run, use this gold to buy new items or new heroes.

Equip Your Heroes

Using the Items you bought equip your heroes with the best gear and fight the hordes of evil that endanger your Kingdom!

Built with Visual Scripting

Six Sided Heroes is built with Unity Visual Scripting. The goal was to create a fully functional Andriod Game with using Unities Visual Scripting as a base. Using the active State Machines to controll the flow of action, the rolling of the various dice and much more!
